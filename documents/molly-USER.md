**Target Properties:**

**Target Sellers:**
- 40+ years old
- Owned land 10+ years
- Inherited via probate
- Tax liens or foreclosure situations

**Acquisition Offers (Two-Option Strategy):**
1. **Cash offer:** 40-50% of market value
2. **Seller financing:** 80-90% of market value (if terms work)
