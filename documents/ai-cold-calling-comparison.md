# AI Cold Calling Solutions for Dirt Roamers Land Business

**Research Date:** February 2026  
**Prepared for:** Dirt Roamers Land Investing  
**Target Audience:** Motivated land sellers (40+ years old, owned land 10+ years, inherited/probate situations)

---

## Executive Summary

This report compares AI cold calling solutions for land investing outreach. The primary focus is **Land AI (landai.ai)** — a specialized solution built specifically for land investors — along with general-purpose AI voice platforms that could be adapted for land investing.

**Key Finding:** Land AI offers the most tailored solution for land investors with a fully managed service model, while platforms like Synthflow and Retell AI provide more flexible, lower-cost options for those willing to DIY setup and management.

---

## 1. Land AI (landai.ai) — PRIMARY FOCUS

### Overview
Land AI is purpose-built for land investors, offering a **fully managed AI cold calling system** with their AI agent "Maya." Unlike general-purpose platforms, Land AI handles the entire acquisitions operation — not just the calling technology.

### How It Works
1. You buy leads (50-200/month) — they run the outreach
2. Maya (AI sales rep) qualifies leads live on calls
3. Human QA reviews every conversation
4. Hot leads routed to your CRM; rest go to automated nurture
5. You only talk to the ~20 qualified sellers, not 100 maybes

### Pricing Structure
| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Setup/Onboarding | **$0** (included in first month) |
| Platform Fee | Custom pricing based on lead volume |
| Lead Plans | 50, 100, or 200 leads per month options |
| Minimum Commitment | **Month-to-month** (discounts for 3/6/12-month prepay) |

**Note:** Land AI doesn't publish exact per-lead pricing publicly — requires sales consultation.

### Features
- ✅ Maya AI agent trained on thousands of land-seller conversations
- ✅ Full 5-minute qualification calls (motivation, price, timeline)
- ✅ **100% human QA review** before leads hit CRM
- ✅ Full transcripts, summaries, and comp reports included
- ✅ DNC scrubbing and compliance (all 50 states)
- ✅ TCPA-compliant (human-dialed → AI warm transfer)
- ✅ Automated nurture for "not yet" leads
- ✅ State registrations and bond filings handled
- ✅ Dedicated Slack workspace for support

### CRM/GoHighLevel Integration
- ✅ **Native GoHighLevel integration**
- ✅ HubSpot, REI Conversion, InvestorFuse, custom CRMs via webhook
- ✅ Leads land in pipeline with full notes, ready for follow-up

### Reported ROI
| Metric | Traditional Cold Calling | With Land AI |
|--------|-------------------------|--------------|
| Deals per 100 leads | ~1 deal | ~5 deals (from 20 qualified) |
| Time spent qualifying | 80+ hours | ~20% of that time |
| First qualified lead | Days/weeks | **Within 24 hours** |

**Claimed Performance:** 5× more effective than cold calling with 80% less time qualifying.

### Pros
- Purpose-built for land investors (not generic AI)
- Fully managed — no tech setup required
- Human QA catches AI mistakes
- Month-to-month flexibility
- Compliance handled for all 50 states
- "Not yet" leads stay in nurture pipeline

### Cons
- Pricing not transparent (must contact sales)
- Less control over AI conversations
- May be more expensive than DIY solutions
- Dependent on their team for changes/customization

### Affiliate/Referral Program
- Not publicly advertised. Would need to inquire directly.

## 2. Air.ai

Air.ai is an enterprise-grade AI voice platform capable of conducting 10-40 minute human-like phone conversations. Primarily targets large sales teams and call centers.

### Pricing Structure
| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Upfront License Fee | **$25,000 - $100,000** (one-time or annual) |
| Outbound Calls | $0.11/minute |
| Inbound Calls | $0.32/minute |
| API/Roleplay | $0.12/minute |

- ✅ Lead qualification and appointment booking
- ✅ Salesforce and HubSpot integration

- ❌ No free trial available
- ❌ Complex enterprise setup

### CRM/GoHighLevel Integration
- Salesforce, HubSpot integration available
- GoHighLevel integration possible but requires custom setup
- Premium integrations may incur additional fees

### Minimum Commitment
- Enterprise agreements required
- Likely annual commitment with license fee

### Pros

- Enterprise-grade reliability

## Final Thoughts

For Dirt Roamers targeting older landowners (40+) who've owned property 10+ years, often in inherited/probate situations, the AI needs to:

- **Sound warm and patient** (not pushy or robotic)
- **Understand land-specific motivation** (carrying costs, inheritance burden, unused property)
- **Ask the right qualification questions** (timeline, price expectations, willingness to negotiate)
- **Handle objections gracefully** (I'm not ready, need to talk to family, etc.)

**Land AI** is designed specifically for this. General platforms like Synthflow can work but require more customization to nail the land investor use case.

The math is simple: If AI calling helps you close **1-2 extra deals per year**, it pays for itself many times over at typical land flip margins.
