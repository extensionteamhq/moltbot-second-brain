## Part 1: Cold Calling Scripts

### Core Principles

Cold calling remains one of the most effective methods to reach motivated sellers in 2025/2026. Key success factors:

1. **Start with accurate data** — Build your own lists vs. buying stale data
2. **Get organized before dialing** — Use a CRM to track conversations
3. **Use proven scripts** — Don't wing it
4. **Track everything** — Notes on every call enable effective follow-ups
5. **Be persistent & consistent** — Volume matters; the more calls, the more deals

### Land-Specific Cold Calling Scripts

#### Script #1: Initial Outreach (Direct Approach)

> "Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] with Dirt Roamers. I'm calling about your property on [Road Name] in [County]. Quick question — is now an okay time to chat for just a minute?"
>
> *(If yes)*
>
> "Great. I'm a local land investor and I've been looking at properties in your area. I was wondering if you've ever thought about selling that land — either now or sometime in the future?"

**Key:** Get straight to the point. Your goal is to assess interest and book a follow-up, not close on the first call.

---

#### Script #2: Curiosity-Based Opener

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm actually looking at a property in [County] and your name came up as the owner. I just had a quick question about the land on [Road/Parcel] — is that still something you own?"
>
> *(If yes)*
>
> "Perfect. The reason I'm calling is I buy land in the area, and I was curious if selling is something you'd consider — either for cash or maybe on terms that work for you?"

**Why it works:** Leading with curiosity and verification lowers defenses.

---

#### Script #3: The "Referral" Approach

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm looking to buy some land in [County] and I was wondering if you or anyone you know might be interested in selling? I came across your property and thought I'd check."

**Why it works:** Gives the seller an easy out (refer someone else) while also allowing them to self-identify as interested.

---

#### Script #4: Follow-Up Call

> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Dirt Roamers. We spoke a few [weeks/months] ago about your property in [County]. I wanted to check back in — has anything changed in your situation? Are you still thinking about selling?"

**Key:** Reference the prior conversation. This is where most deals close — in the follow-up.

---

#### Script #5: Probate/Inherited Land

> "Hi, I'm looking for [Name]. My name is [Your Name] and I buy vacant land in [County]. I understand you may have recently inherited some property, and I know dealing with inherited land can be a hassle. If you're open to it, I'd love to make you a fair offer and handle all the details. Would that be something worth discussing?"

**Why it works:** Acknowledges the situation, offers help, low-pressure.

---

### Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I'm not interested" | "No problem at all. Would it be okay if I checked back in a few months in case things change?" |
| "How much are you offering?" | "It depends on a few things — let me ask a couple quick questions so I can put together a fair number for you." |
| "I'm not sure I want to sell" | "Totally understand. Most people I talk to aren't sure at first. Can I send you some information and follow up in a few weeks?" |
| "I want full market value" | "I get it. I actually have two ways to structure an offer — one is cash (quick close, less hassle) and one is on terms that could get you closer to full price. Want me to run both options for you?" |
| "How did you get my number?" | "I use public records to find property owners. I know it's unexpected, but I genuinely am looking to buy land in the area." |

## Part 2: SMS/Text Message Templates

### ⚠️ Critical SMS Compliance Note

**Carrier filtering has gotten strict.** Avoid these words in your *first* cold text:
- Interested, Selling, Offer, Property, Cash, Local Investor, Purchase, Looking, Mortgage, Loan, Debt

**Best practice:** Lead with curiosity, not intent. Once they reply, the conversation opens up.

---

### SMS Script #1: Initial Outreach (Curiosity-Based)

> "Hi, is this the owner of the property on [Road Name] in [County]?"

**Why it works:** Simple, curious, non-spammy. Gets a response.

---

### SMS Script #2: Follow-Up After Response

Once they confirm ownership:

> "Great! I was wondering if you're still interested in selling it? I'm looking to buy land in the area."

Or the open-ended version:

> "Thanks! When might you be interested in selling? I'm actively buying in [County]."

---

### SMS Script #3: The Referral Play

> "Hey! I'm looking to buy some land in [County]. Do you know anyone who might want to sell? My name is [Name] — feel free to share my number."

**Why it works:** Non-invasive, but the recipient often says "I might be interested" themselves.

---

### SMS Script #4: Follow-Up Sequence

**Day 3:**
> "Hi [Name], just wanted to follow up on my message about your property. Still interested in chatting?"

**Day 7:**
> "Hey, wanted to check in one more time. If selling isn't on your radar, no worries at all!"

**Day 14:**
> "Last reach-out for now. If things change down the road, feel free to text me anytime. — [Name]"

---

### SMS Best Practices

1. **Use variation** — Don't send the same message to everyone; carriers flag it as spam
2. **Keep it short** — Under 160 characters when possible
3. **Include your name** — Builds trust
4. **Follow up with a call** — Texts start conversations; calls close deals
5. **Respect opt-outs** — If they say stop, stop immediately

---

### What to Look for in a Cold Calling VA

1. **Real estate experience** — Ideally land or wholesaling background
2. **Script adherence** — Can follow your scripts while sounding natural
3. **CRM proficiency** — Familiar with GoHighLevel, Podio, REsimpli, etc.
4. **English fluency** — Clear communication is critical
5. **Persistence** — Understands it's a numbers game
6. **Reporting** — Provides daily/weekly call metrics

### Recommended VA Workflow

1. **Pull list** from Land Portal or similar
2. **Skip trace** for phone numbers
3. **VA makes calls** using your scripts (aim for 100-200 dials/day)
4. **VA logs everything** in GoHighLevel
5. **Warm leads transferred** to you for follow-up/offer
6. **You close the deal**

## Part 4: Recommended Action Plan

### Phase 1: Test Scripts Yourself (Now)

Before hiring a VA:
1. Pull 50-100 leads from Land Portal
2. Make calls yourself for 1-2 weeks
3. Test scripts, refine based on responses
4. Track metrics: dials, contacts, conversations, offers

### Phase 2: Hire Part-Time VA (Once Scripts Work)

1. Start with TaskBullet or similar flexible option
2. Train VA on your proven scripts
3. Have VA make 100+ dials/day
4. You handle all warm leads and closings
5. Evaluate ROI after 30 days

### Phase 3: Scale with Full-Time VA (Deal Flow Proven)

1. Upgrade to REVA Global or MyOutDesk
2. VA handles all cold outreach + lead management
3. You focus 100% on acquisitions and dispositions
4. Consider adding second VA or AI calling
