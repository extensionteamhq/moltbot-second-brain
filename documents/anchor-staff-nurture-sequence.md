# Anchor & Staff Nurture Email Sequence
## "The Revenue Foundation Series"

**Target Audience:** Small business owners who don't understand the value of branding and need education on marketing fundamentals to grow their business.

**Tone:** Educational, generous with value, empathetic to their overwhelm. Position Anchor & Staff as the guide who can do it for them when they're ready.

---

## Email 1: Welcome + The Real Problem
**Subject:** Why working harder isn't growing your business
**Day 0**

The real problem isn't that you're not working hard enough. It's that you're drowning in tactics without strategy.

**Series Overview:**
1. Clarity — A brand message that resonates
2. Content — Becoming the authority
3. Capture — Systems that turn visitors into leads
4. Convert — Paid strategies that scale

---

## Email 2: The StoryBrand Foundation
**Subject:** Your customer is the hero (not you)
**Day 2**

The StoryBrand 7-Part Framework:
1. A CHARACTER (your customer)
2. Has a PROBLEM
3. Meets a GUIDE (you)
4. Who gives them a PLAN
5. Calls them to ACTION
6. Helps them avoid FAILURE
7. Ends in SUCCESS

**DIY Action:** Answer who your customer is, their problem, your plan, success outcome, and failure avoided.

---

## Email 3: Your Website as a Sales Machine
**Subject:** Your website has 5 seconds to work
**Day 4**

Above the fold needs:
- Clear header (transformation statement)
- Subheader (how/who)
- One CTA button
- Success image

**Formula:** "[Desired outcome] + [without/with] + [pain avoided or benefit gained]"

---

## Email 4: Content That Builds Authority
**Subject:** How to become the obvious choice
**Day 7**

Content Hierarchy:
1. Social Media (Awareness)
2. Blog/SEO (Discovery)
3. Email Newsletter (Nurture)
4. Lead Magnets (Capture)
5. PR & Podcasts (Credibility)

**DIY:** Pick ONE channel, master it. Post your top 10 FAQs as content.

---

## Email 5: Lead Magnets That Convert
**Subject:** The "ethical bribe" that builds your pipeline
**Day 10**

Great lead magnets:
- Solve ONE specific problem
- Provide quick wins
- Lead naturally to your paid offer

Ideas: Checklists, assessments, guides, templates, quizzes

---

## Email 6: Paid Media Foundations
**Subject:** When to pour gas on the fire
**Day 14**

Only after foundation is solid. Hierarchy:
1. Search Ads (capture demand)
2. Social Ads (create demand)
3. Retargeting (stay top of mind)
4. Streaming/Audio (brand building)

**Formula:** Ad → Lead Magnet → Email Nurture → Sales

---

## Email 7: The Complete Revenue System
**Subject:** The system that works while you sleep
**Day 18**

```
AWARENESS → CAPTURE → NURTURE → CONVERT → DELIGHT → REPEAT
```

Audit your system at each stage. Find the weakest link.

---

## Email 8: The Invitation
**Subject:** Ready when you are
**Day 21**

Recap all emails. Offer Revenue Roadmap for those ready for help.

*"Strategy without execution is hallucination. Execution without strategy is chaos."*
