# SOP: Customer Retention Process
## Anchor & Staff Agency

### Purpose
Maximize client lifetime value through exceptional delivery, proactive communication, and strategic expansion.

---

## 1. Retention Philosophy

### Core Principles
- **Deliver results** — Retention starts with outcomes
- **Proactive communication** — Don't wait for problems
- **Anticipate needs** — Suggest next steps before they ask
- **Build relationships** — Know their business, not just their project
- **Make it easy** — Remove friction at every touchpoint

---

## 2. Client Success Framework

### Success Milestones
| Milestone | Timing | Action |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Kickoff | Day 1 | Set expectations, build rapport |
| First Win | Week 2-4 | Deliver early value, celebrate |
| Mid-Project | Halfway | Check-in, gather feedback |
| Delivery | Completion | Review results, discuss next steps |
| Post-Project | 30 days | Follow up on implementation |
| Quarterly | Ongoing | Business review, expansion discussion |

### Success Metrics by Service
| Service | Success Metric |
|---------|----------------|
| Revenue Roadmap | Clarity achieved, action plan in place |
| Brand Strategy | Consistent messaging implemented |
| Website | Traffic/conversion improvement |
| Retainer | Leads generated, revenue attributed |

---

## 3. Proactive Communication

### Regular Touchpoints
- **Weekly:** Status update during active projects
- **Bi-weekly:** Check-in call for retainer clients
- **Monthly:** Performance report with insights
- **Quarterly:** Business review and planning

### Ad-Hoc Communication
- Share relevant articles or insights
- Congratulate on business wins (PR, funding, etc.)
- Alert to industry changes that affect them
- Introduce to helpful connections

### Communication Triggers
- Project milestone completed → Send update
- Metric improvement → Share the win
- Potential issue spotted → Proactive outreach
- Haven't heard from them → Check in

---

## 4. Feedback Collection

### During Projects
- Ask for feedback at each milestone
- "What's working well? What could be better?"
- Act on feedback immediately when possible

### Project Completion
- Formal feedback survey (NPS + open questions)
- Debrief call to discuss experience
- Request testimonial if satisfied

### Ongoing Clients
- Quarterly satisfaction check
- Annual business review
- Track NPS over time

### Survey Questions
1. How likely are you to recommend us? (0-10)
2. What did we do well?
3. What could we improve?
4. Would you work with us again?
5. Any other feedback?

---

## 5. Issue Resolution

### Response Protocol
1. Acknowledge issue within 4 hours
2. Investigate root cause
3. Propose solution within 24 hours
4. Implement fix
5. Follow up to confirm resolution
6. Document for future prevention

### Escalation Triggers
- Client expresses frustration twice
- Issue unresolved after 48 hours
- Financial or legal implications
- Risk of client churn

### Recovery Actions
- Partner-level outreach
- Goodwill gesture (extra deliverable, discount)
- Root cause analysis
- Process improvement

---

## 6. Client Expansion

### Expansion Opportunities
| Current Service | Natural Next Step |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Revenue Roadmap | Implementation retainer |
| Brand Strategy | Website redesign |
| Website | SEO/Content retainer |
| One-time project | Ongoing retainer |

### Expansion Signals
- "What would you recommend next?"
- New business challenge mentioned
- Growth/expansion happening
- Budget discussions

### Expansion Conversation
1. Review results achieved
2. Discuss current goals and challenges
3. Present relevant next steps
4. Propose specific engagement
5. Handle questions
6. Close or set follow-up

---

## 7. Renewal Process

### Retainer Renewals
- Begin conversation 60 days before renewal
- Review results and ROI
- Discuss goals for next period
- Propose renewal terms
- Adjust scope/pricing if needed

### Renewal Meeting Agenda
1. Results recap
2. What worked well
3. What to improve
4. Goals for next period
5. Proposed scope and investment
6. Timeline for decision

---

## 8. Churn Prevention

### Warning Signs
- Decreased engagement or responsiveness
- Complaints or negative feedback
- Budget concerns mentioned
- Key contact leaving
- Change in business direction

### Intervention Actions
1. Proactive check-in call
2. Address concerns directly
3. Demonstrate recent value/wins
4. Offer solutions (scope adjustment, pause, etc.)
5. Partner involvement if needed

### Exit Interviews
- Always conduct when client leaves
- Ask why and what could have prevented it
- Document learnings
- Leave door open for future

---

## 9. Client Appreciation

### Recognition Programs
- Shout out client wins on social (with permission)
- Case study features
- Referral rewards
- Anniversary acknowledgments

### Appreciation Gestures
- Thank you notes (handwritten when possible)
- Holiday gifts
- Business milestone celebrations
- Introductions to valuable connections

---

## 10. Retention Metrics

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Net Promoter Score | 50+ |
| Client retention rate | 85%+ |
| Expansion revenue | 20% of revenue |
| Average client tenure | 12+ months |

### Tracking
- Log all client interactions
- Track NPS over time
- Monitor engagement levels
- Review churn reasons quarterly
