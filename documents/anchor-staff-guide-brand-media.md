### What Is a Brand Media Style Guide?

A Brand Media Style Guide defines how your brand appears in photography and videoâ€”covering everything from lighting and composition to wardrobe and editing style. It ensures visual consistency across all media touchpoints, from social media posts to professional video productions.

**Why It Matters:**
- Consistent imagery builds recognition faster than any other element
- Anyone creating content (internal or external) can match your brand's look
- Prevents the "stock photo feel" that makes brands look generic
- Protects your investment in professional photo/video shoots
- Creates a cohesive story across platforms and campaigns

**Who Uses This Guide:**
- Photographers (in-house and freelance)
- Videographers and video editors
- Social media managers
- Content creators and influencers
- Creative directors
- Marketing team members
- External agencies and production houses
- Brand ambassadors creating UGC

## Section 1: Media Philosophy

### What to Include

The overarching vision that guides all photo and video decisions.

### 1.1 Visual Story Summary

**What It Is:** A brief statement describing the feeling and story your media should convey.

**Deliverable Format:**
> **Our Media Vision**
> 
> [2-3 paragraphs describing what your media should make people feel, the story it tells, and how it connects to your brand personality. This is the "north star" for all creative decisions.]
>
> **We want people to feel:** [Primary emotion]
>
> **Our media tells the story of:** [Core narrative]
>
> **Every image/video should reinforce:** [Key brand truth]

**Example:**
> "Our media captures the moment when someone realizes they can do thisâ€”when complexity becomes clarity, when overwhelm becomes a plan. We show real people in real moments of progress, not polished perfection.
>
> We want people to feel **empowered and capable**.
>
> Our media tells the story of **transformation through partnership**â€”the journey from stuck to scaling.
>
> Every image/video should reinforce: **You don't have to figure this out alone.**"

### 1.2 Media Principles

**What It Is:** The 3-5 guiding rules for all photography and video.

**Deliverable Format:**

> **Our media principles:**
>
> 1. **Authenticity over polish** â€” Real moments beat staged perfection
> 2. **People over products** â€” Human connection is central to every frame
> 3. **Story over shot** â€” Every image should imply a before and after
> 4. **Consistency over creativity** â€” Stay on-brand even when trying something new
> 5. **Purposeful over decorative** â€” If it doesn't reinforce the message, cut it

### 1.3 Media Personality Alignment

**What It Is:** How your brand personality traits translate into visual media.

**Deliverable Format:**

| Brand Trait | Photography Expression | Video Expression |
|-------------|----------------------|------------------|

## Section 7: Production Checklist Templates

### 7.1 Photo Shoot Checklist

```markdown

- [ ] Creative brief approved
- [ ] Shot list created
- [ ] Location scouted and confirmed
- [ ] Subjects confirmed and prepped on wardrobe
- [ ] Equipment list finalized
- [ ] Permits/permissions secured (if needed)

## Day of Shoot
- [ ] Arrive early to set up
- [ ] Test lighting before subjects arrive
```
