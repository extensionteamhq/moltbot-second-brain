## Posting Best Practices

### Twitter/X
- **Timing:** 8-10am ET (morning scroll), 12-1pm ET (lunch), 5-7pm ET (evening commute)
- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day
- **Format:** Short, punchy, conversational. Use emojis. Ask questions.
- **Hashtags:** 1-3 per post max (#LandInvesting, #RealEstate, #Homesteading, #DirtRoamers)
- **Engagement:** Reply to comments within 1-2 hours. Quote-tweet relevant industry posts.

### LinkedIn
- **Timing:** 7-9am ET (before work), 12-1pm ET (lunch break), 5-6pm ET (end of workday)
- **Frequency:** 3-5 posts per week (weekdays focus)
- **Format:** Longer-form, educational, professional. Use line breaks for readability.
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 per post (#LandInvestment, #RealEstateInvesting, #Homesteading, #PassiveIncome, #WealthBuilding)
- **Engagement:** Respond to all comments. Post in relevant groups (real estate investing, land investment, homesteading).
