# Growth Market Data Sources

*Researched: 2026-02-05*

---

## Overview

Finding "hot" markets for land investing requires analyzing:
- Population growth and migration patterns
- Economic indicators (jobs, income)
- Infrastructure development
- Housing demand trends
- Demographic shifts

This research covers tools and data sources for identifying growth markets analytically.

### Major Outflow Areas
- California metros: -22.3% net population (YoY)

### Land Classification Opportunities
- **Transitional agricultural land** (in growth corridors): Current ag income + future development
- **Waterfront land:** Consistent demand, limited supply
- **Mountain properties:** Remote worker demand, recreation + timber income

## Free Data Sources

### 1. U.S. Census Bureau

**Website:** https://data.census.gov

#### American Community Survey (ACS)
- Population demographics
- Income and employment
- Housing characteristics
- Migration flows (county-to-county)

**Migration Flows API:** https://www.census.gov/data/developers/data-sets/acs-migration-flows.html
- Shows where people are moving FROM and TO
- 5-year data by county, MCD, and metro area

#### How to Use
1. Go to data.census.gov
2. Search for your target counties
3. Look at:
   - Population change (5-year trend)
   - Median household income
   - Housing units and vacancy rates
   - Migration inflow/outflow

**Cost:** FREE

---

### 2. National Association of Realtors (NAR) Migration Trends

**Website:** https://www.nar.realtor/research-and-statistics/research-reports/migration-trends

#### What They Offer
- National, regional, and metro-level statistics
- Research reports on migration patterns
- Housing statistics by market

**Cost:** FREE (some reports may require membership)

---

### 3. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)

**Website:** https://www.bls.gov

#### What to Look For
- Employment trends by county
- Industry growth/decline
- Wage data

**Why It Matters:** Job growth = population growth = land demand

**Cost:** FREE

## DIY Market Analysis Framework

### Step 1: Identify Candidate Markets
Use free data to screen:
1. **Census ACS:** Population growth > 5% (5-year)
2. **BLS:** Job growth positive
3. **NAR:** Housing demand indicators

### Step 2: Deep Dive Analysis
For each candidate:
1. **Migration flow:** Net inflow or outflow?
2. **Income trends:** Rising median income?
3. **Housing inventory:** Low supply = opportunity
4. **Infrastructure:** New roads, hospitals, schools planned?

### Step 3: On-the-Ground Validation
1. **Land Portal/LandInsights:** Pull data for target counties
2. **Zillow/Redfin:** Check days on market, price trends
3. **Local news:** Search "[County] development" or "[County] growth"
4. **Google Earth:** Visual check of development patterns
