# SOP: Operations Process
## Anchor & Staff Agency

### Purpose
Establish systems for efficient project delivery, resource management, and operational excellence.

---

## 1. Project Management Framework

### Project Lifecycle
1. **Kickoff** — Align on goals, timeline, stakeholders
2. **Discovery** — Research, audits, data gathering
3. **Strategy** — Analysis, recommendations, roadmap
4. **Execution** — Implementation of deliverables
5. **Review** — Client feedback, revisions
6. **Delivery** — Final handoff, documentation
7. **Close** — Retrospective, testimonial request

### Project Types & Timelines
| Project | Duration | Phases |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Revenue Roadmap | 2-3 weeks | Discovery → Strategy → Delivery |
| Brand Strategy | 4-6 weeks | Discovery → Strategy → Guidelines |
| Website Build | 6-10 weeks | Strategy → Design → Development → Launch |
| Ongoing Retainer | Monthly | Planning → Execution → Reporting |

---

## 2. Client Onboarding

### Day 0: Contract Signed
- [ ] Send welcome email with next steps
- [ ] Create client folder in project management tool
- [ ] Set up communication channel (Slack/email)
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call
- [ ] Send onboarding questionnaire

### Kickoff Call Agenda (60 min)
1. Introductions and roles
2. Review goals and success metrics
3. Confirm timeline and milestones
4. Discuss communication preferences
5. Gather access (analytics, accounts, etc.)
6. Answer questions
7. Confirm next steps

### Onboarding Questionnaire Topics
- Business overview and history
- Current marketing activities
- Target audience description
- Competitors
- Brand assets (logos, fonts, colors)
- Access credentials needed
- Key stakeholders and approval process

---

## 3. Communication Standards

### Response Times
- Email: Within 24 hours (business days)
- Slack/Chat: Within 4 hours (business hours)
- Urgent issues: Same day

### Meeting Cadence
| Project Type | Frequency | Duration |
|--------------|-----------|----------|
| Revenue Roadmap | Weekly | 30 min |
| Implementation | Weekly | 45 min |
| Retainer | Bi-weekly | 45 min |

### Meeting Best Practices
- Always have an agenda
- Send recap with action items within 24 hours
- Record calls (with permission) for reference
- Start and end on time

### Status Updates
- Weekly written update for active projects
- Include: Completed, In Progress, Blockers, Next Week

---

## 4. Project Management Tools

### Recommended Stack
- **Project Management:** Notion, ClickUp, Asana, or Monday
- **Communication:** Slack (internal), Email (client)
- **File Storage:** Google Drive or Dropbox
- **Time Tracking:** Toggl, Harvest, or Clockify
- **Documentation:** Notion or Google Docs

### Folder Structure
```
/Clients
  /[Client Name]
    /01-Admin (contracts, invoices)
    /02-Discovery (questionnaires, research)
    /03-Strategy (decks, roadmaps)
    /04-Creative (designs, copy)
    /05-Assets (logos, images)
    /06-Reports (analytics, results)
```

---

## 5. Quality Assurance

### Before Client Delivery
- [ ] Proofread all copy
- [ ] Check all links work
- [ ] Verify data accuracy
- [ ] Review against original brief
- [ ] Internal peer review
- [ ] Test on multiple devices (if applicable)

### Revision Process
- Clearly define revision rounds in scope
- Track feedback in single document
- Confirm approval in writing before final delivery

---

## 6. Resource Management

### Capacity Planning
- Track hours per project
- Review utilization weekly
- Flag overallocation early
- Balance workload across team

### Contractor Management
- Maintain vetted contractor roster
- Clear SOWs for contractor work
- Review deliverables before client handoff
- Pay within agreed terms

---

## 7. Workflow Templates

### Revenue Roadmap Workflow
1. Kickoff call (Day 1)
2. Send questionnaire (Day 1)
3. Receive completed questionnaire (Day 3)
4. Competitive research (Day 4-5)
5. Customer research (Day 6-7)
6. Draft roadmap (Day 8-10)
7. Internal review (Day 11)
8. Client presentation (Day 12-14)
9. Revisions (Day 15-17)
10. Final delivery (Day 18-21)

### Website Project Workflow
1. Kickoff & discovery (Week 1)
2. Sitemap & wireframes (Week 2)
3. Design mockups (Week 3-4)
4. Client feedback & revisions (Week 5)
5. Development (Week 6-8)
6. Content population (Week 8-9)
7. QA & testing (Week 9)
8. Launch (Week 10)

---

## 8. Risk Management

### Common Risks & Mitigation
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Scope creep | Clear SOW, change request process |
| Client delays | Buffer in timeline, escalation path |
| Resource gaps | Cross-train team, contractor bench |
| Tech failures | Backups, redundant systems |

### Escalation Path
1. Project lead attempts resolution
2. Escalate to partner if unresolved
3. Client call to realign if needed
4. Document everything

---

## 9. Documentation Standards

### Project Documentation
- Keep meeting notes in project folder
- Document all decisions and approvals
- Create handoff guides for deliverables
- Archive completed projects

### Process Documentation
- Maintain SOPs for repeatable tasks
- Update SOPs when processes change
- Train team on new procedures

---

## 10. Operational Metrics

### Efficiency Metrics
- Average project delivery time
- Revision rounds per project
- Client satisfaction score

### Utilization Metrics
- Billable vs. non-billable hours
- Team utilization rate (target: 70-80%)
- Project profitability

### Quality Metrics
- Defects/issues per project
- On-time delivery rate
- Scope change frequency
