## What to Look For

### Must-Have Capabilities
- **Assignments** — Wholesale deals
- **Double Closings** — Buy and sell same day
- **Single-Source Funding** — Use end buyer's funds for both transactions
- **Seller Financing Documents** — Notes, deeds of trust, land contracts
- **Proactive Communication** — Don't let deals die
- **Title Issue Resolution** — Actively solve problems

### Nice-to-Have
- Loan servicing department
- Investor volume pricing
- Remote/mail-away closings
- Multi-state coverage

## Cost Expectations

| Deal Size | Approach | Expected Cost |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Under $5K | Self-closing or hybrid | $50-300 |
| $5K-$15K | Title company | $300-700 |
| $15K-$50K | Full title company | $700-1,200 |
| $50K+ | Full with enhanced coverage | $1,200-2,000+ |

---

## Red Flags

⚠️ "We don't do land closings"
⚠️ "What's a double closing?"
⚠️ Slow communication (3+ days)
⚠️ Surprise fees
⚠️ No problem-solving attitude
