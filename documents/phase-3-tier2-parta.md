Pricing Justification
Market Comparison:


* Pay-Per-Lead Services (HomeAdvisor, Angi): $15-80 per lead, shared with 3-5 competitors
* Marketing Agencies: $1,500-$5,000/month for SEO services, no guaranteed leads
* Google Ads (DIY): $500-$3,000/month with uncertain ROI
* Our Service: $800-$1,500/month, exclusive leads, predictable cost


Value Proposition:


* Client receives 15-50 exclusive leads monthly
* Market value: $750-$4,000 (at $50/lead average)
* Client pays: $800-$1,500
* ROI: 1:1 to 5:1 on lead value alone (before considering conversion to jobs)


Typical Client Economics:


* Average lead-to-job conversion: 20-30%
* Average job value: $3,000-$15,000 (varies by niche)
* Jobs closed per month: 3-15 (from 15-50 leads)
* Monthly revenue from our leads: $9,000-$225,000
* Our fee as % of revenue: 0.7% to 16% (tiny fraction of generated business)
