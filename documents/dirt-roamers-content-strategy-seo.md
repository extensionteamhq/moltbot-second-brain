# Content Strategy & SEO for Dirt Roamers Resources

*Created: February 14, 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

This guide provides a comprehensive content strategy for building the Dirt Roamers Resources educational arm. The goal: attract aspiring land investors through valuable content, capture leads with irresistible lead magnets, and monetize through affiliate commissions and digital products.

**Key Insight:** Land flipping content is a "Blue Ocean" ‚Äî far less competitive than house flipping, wholesaling, or rental property content. Most existing content is mediocre or outdated. Quality content will rank.

---

## Part 1: Content Pillars

Structure all content around five core pillars that map to the buyer journey:

### Pillar 1: Finding Deals (Top of Funnel)
**Intent:** "How do I find land deals?"

Topics include:
- Data sources and list building
- Skip tracing strategies
- Market selection criteria
- Identifying motivated sellers
- County records research

**Keyword themes:** how to find land deals, vacant land leads, land investor data sources

### Pillar 2: Analyzing & Offering (Middle of Funnel)
**Intent:** "How do I evaluate and make offers?"

Topics include:
- Running comps on land
- Due diligence checklist
- Offer pricing formulas
- Two-offer strategy (cash vs. terms)
- Negotiation techniques

**Keyword themes:** land deal analysis, how to comp vacant land, land due diligence

### Pillar 3: Selling Land (Middle of Funnel)
**Intent:** "How do I sell the land for profit?"

Topics include:
- Listing platforms (LandWatch, Facebook, etc.)
- Creating compelling listings
- Seller financing to buyers
- Marketing land properties
- Building a buyer list

**Keyword themes:** how to sell land fast, land listing platforms, seller financing land

### Pillar 4: Tools & Tech (Bottom of Funnel)
**Intent:** "What tools do I need?"

Topics include:
- Software reviews (Land Portal, PropStream, etc.)
- CRM setup (GoHighLevel)
- Skip tracing tools
- AI calling tools
- Comparison guides

**Keyword themes:** best land investing software, land portal review, gohighlevel for land investors

### Pillar 5: Lifestyle & Mindset (Brand Building)
**Intent:** "Why land? Is this for me?"

Topics include:
- Why land beats houses
- Passive income through land
- Work from anywhere lifestyle
- Homesteading vision
- Success stories

**Keyword themes:** land flipping lifestyle, passive income land, why flip land
