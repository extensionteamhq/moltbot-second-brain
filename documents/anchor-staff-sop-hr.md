# SOP: Human Resources Process
## Anchor & Staff Agency

### Purpose
Establish best practices for hiring, onboarding, managing, and retaining talent at a brand strategy and digital marketing agency.

---

## 1. Hiring Process

### 1.1 Role Definition
- Create detailed job description with responsibilities, required skills, and outcomes
- Define culture fit criteria aligned with agency values
- Set compensation range based on market research

### 1.2 Sourcing Candidates
- Post on LinkedIn, Indeed, and industry-specific job boards
- Leverage network referrals (offer referral bonuses)
- Consider freelance-to-hire for specialized roles
- Use portfolio reviews for creative positions

### 1.3 Interview Process
1. **Resume/Portfolio Screen** — 15 min review
2. **Phone Screen** — 20 min culture/basics check
3. **Skills Assessment** — Practical test (e.g., mock strategy brief, campaign audit)
4. **Team Interview** — 45 min with hiring manager + team member
5. **Final Interview** — Partner-level conversation
6. **Reference Check** — 2-3 professional references

### 1.4 Offer & Negotiation
- Extend offer within 48 hours of final interview
- Include compensation, benefits, start date, and 90-day expectations
- Allow 3-5 business days for decision

---

## 2. Onboarding Process

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Set up email, Slack, project management tools
- [ ] Assign onboarding buddy
- [ ] Complete HR paperwork and compliance training
- [ ] Review agency handbook and policies
- [ ] Meet all team members (15-min 1:1s)
- [ ] Overview of current clients and projects

### Week 2-4: Immersion
- [ ] Shadow client calls and internal meetings
- [ ] Complete role-specific training modules
- [ ] Assign first small project with mentor oversight
- [ ] Weekly check-ins with manager
- [ ] Review agency methodologies (StoryBrand, Value Ladder, etc.)

### Day 30: Check-in
- Formal 30-day review
- Feedback both ways
- Adjust training plan if needed

### Day 90: Evaluation
- Performance review against initial expectations
- Confirm continued employment or address concerns
- Set goals for next quarter

---

## 3. Performance Management

### Quarterly Reviews
- Self-assessment + manager assessment
- Review OKRs/goals from previous quarter
- Set new goals for upcoming quarter
- Discuss career development

### Continuous Feedback
- Weekly 1:1s (30 min minimum)
- Real-time feedback on project work
- Celebrate wins publicly, address issues privately

### Performance Improvement
1. Verbal coaching (documented)
2. Written warning with clear expectations
3. Performance Improvement Plan (30-60 days)
4. Termination if no improvement

---

## 4. Compensation & Benefits

### Salary Reviews
- Annual review aligned with performance
- Market adjustment every 2 years
- Promotion paths clearly defined

### Benefits (Recommended)
- Health insurance (or stipend for contractors)
- Flexible PTO (minimum 15 days)
- Remote work flexibility
- Professional development budget ($1,000-2,000/year)
- Conference attendance opportunities

---

## 5. Offboarding Process

### Voluntary Departure
- [ ] Conduct exit interview
- [ ] Knowledge transfer plan (2 weeks minimum)
- [ ] Transition client relationships
- [ ] Revoke system access on last day
- [ ] Final paycheck and benefits info

### Involuntary Departure
- [ ] Document reasons thoroughly
- [ ] Legal review if needed
- [ ] Conduct with dignity and respect
- [ ] Immediate system access revocation
- [ ] Team communication plan

---

## 6. Culture & Engagement

### Core Values (Define Yours)
- Example: Clarity over complexity
- Example: Outcomes over activity
- Example: Generosity with knowledge

### Team Building
- Monthly team meetings (virtual or in-person)
- Quarterly team activities
- Annual retreat/planning session

### Recognition
- Shout-outs in team meetings
- Client win celebrations
- Milestone acknowledgments (work anniversaries, etc.)

---

## Key Metrics
- Time to hire (target: <30 days)
- 90-day retention rate (target: >90%)
- Employee satisfaction score (quarterly pulse surveys)
- Referral hire percentage
