# SOP: Marketing Process
## Anchor & Staff Agency

### Purpose
Define how the agency markets itself to attract ideal clients consistently.

---

## 1. Brand Positioning

### Core Message
- **Tagline:** "Stop Guessing. Start Generating Revenue."
- **Positioning:** Revenue-focused brand strategy for businesses ready to scale
- **Differentiator:** Strategy + execution combined (not just advice)

### Ideal Client Profile
- Small business owners ($500K-$5M revenue)
- Struggling with brand clarity and marketing ROI
- Ready to invest in strategic growth
- Values partnership over vendor relationships

---

## 2. Content Marketing

### Content Pillars
1. **Brand Strategy** — StoryBrand, positioning, messaging
2. **Revenue Systems** — Funnels, automation, conversion
3. **Marketing Tactics** — Ads, SEO, social, email
4. **Agency Insights** — Behind-the-scenes, case studies

### Content Calendar
| Day | Platform | Content Type |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Educational post |
| Tue | Email | Newsletter |
| Wed | LinkedIn | Story/Case study |
| Thu | Blog | Long-form article |
| Fri | LinkedIn | POV/Hot take |

### Content Creation Process
1. **Ideation** — Weekly brainstorm (Friday)
2. **Outline** — Brief with key points
3. **Draft** — First version
4. **Edit** — Review for clarity and brand voice
5. **Design** — Add visuals if needed
6. **Schedule** — Queue in scheduling tool
7. **Engage** — Respond to comments within 24 hours

---

## 3. Lead Generation

### Inbound Channels
- **Website SEO** — Target "brand strategy agency," "revenue marketing"
- **Content Marketing** — Blog, LinkedIn, newsletter
- **Lead Magnets** — Revenue Roadmap preview, assessments
- **Referrals** — Client referral program

### Outbound Channels (Optional)
- LinkedIn outreach to ideal prospects
- Strategic partnerships with complementary services
- Speaking engagements and podcasts

### Lead Magnet Strategy
- **Primary:** Free brand assessment quiz
- **Secondary:** "Revenue Foundation" email series
- **Conversion Path:** Lead magnet → Nurture sequence → Discovery call CTA

---

## 4. Nurture Sequences

### New Subscriber Sequence
8-email "Revenue Foundation Series" (see separate document)
- Educates on brand/marketing fundamentals
- Positions agency as expert guide
- Soft CTA to Revenue Roadmap

### Re-engagement Sequence
For subscribers inactive 60+ days:
1. "Still interested in growing your revenue?"
2. Share recent case study
3. Final "should we part ways?" email

---

## 5. Website Optimization

### Homepage Must-Haves
- Clear header (transformation statement)
- Problem agitation section
- Solution overview
- Social proof (testimonials, logos)
- Clear CTA above and below fold

### Conversion Points
- Revenue Roadmap request form
- Lead magnet opt-ins
- Newsletter signup
- Contact form

### Monthly Website Tasks
- [ ] Review analytics (traffic, bounce rate, conversions)
- [ ] Update testimonials/case studies
- [ ] Check all forms are working
- [ ] Refresh hero section if needed

---

## 6. Social Proof & Case Studies

### Testimonial Collection
- Request at project completion
- Use specific results when possible
- Video testimonials > written
- Update website quarterly

### Case Study Format
1. **Client Overview** — Industry, size, challenge
2. **The Problem** — What they were struggling with
3. **The Solution** — What we did
4. **The Results** — Specific metrics and outcomes
5. **Client Quote** — In their words

---

## 7. Metrics & Reporting

### Weekly Metrics
- Website traffic
- Lead magnet downloads
- Email open/click rates
- Social engagement

### Monthly Metrics
- New leads generated
- Discovery calls booked
- Proposals sent
- Clients closed
- Revenue from marketing efforts

### Quarterly Review
- What's working? Double down.
- What's not? Stop or fix.
- New experiments to test.

---

## Tools Stack
- **Website:** Next.js/Vercel or WordPress
- **Email:** ConvertKit, Mailchimp, or GoHighLevel
- **Social Scheduling:** Buffer, Hootsuite, or native
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, Plausible
- **CRM:** GoHighLevel, HubSpot, or Notion
