THE OPPORTUNITY
A proven entrepreneur with 24 years of web development experience and 10+ years in professional SEO seeks $60,000 in private money lending to launch a rank-and-rent website business generating recurring monthly revenue from local service providers.


The Model: Build SEO-optimized websites for high-value home services (deck building, foundation repair, window replacement, etc.), rank them in Google search results, then rent these lead-generating assets to qualified local contractors on a month-to-month subscription basis.


The Edge: Unlike competitors using outdated WordPress technology, this business leverages modern web architecture (Next.js, Vercel) for superior performance, combined with proven marketing frameworks (StoryBrand, Russell Brunson, Dan Kennedy) and a zero-cost customer acquisition strategy.

PAGE 2: BUSINESS MODEL OVERVIEW
HOW RANK & RENT WORKS
The rank-and-rent business model creates recurring revenue by building digital assets that generate qualified leads for local service providers. Unlike traditional marketing agencies that charge for ongoing services, this model creates owned assets with intrinsic value.


________________


THE RANK & RENT PROCESS
[VISUAL: Business Model Flow Diagram - See Separate Artifact]
Phase 1: Build (Months 1-2 per site)
1. Market Research: Identify high-value service niche with sufficient search volume
2. Domain Acquisition: Purchase exact-match or branded domain ($20)
3. Website Development: Deploy from master template, customize for niche (1-2 weeks)
4. Content Creation: Write SEO-optimized pages targeting buyer-intent keywords
5. Technical Setup: Implement schema markup, local SEO elements, conversion tracking


Investment per Site: $920 (one-time development cost)


________________


Phase 2: Rank (Months 2-6 average)
1. On-Page Optimization: Title tags, meta descriptions, header structure, internal linking
2. Content Marketing: Regular blog posts, service pages, FAQ content
3. Local Citations: Submit to 20-30 relevant directories and listing sites
4. Link Building: White-hat backlink acquisition from relevant sources
5. Technical Monitoring: Track rankings, fix issues, optimize performance


Monthly Investment per Site: $170 (ongoing SEO maintenance)


Timeline: 6 months average to reach page 1 rankings (conservative estimate; often 3-4 months)


________________


Phase 3: Rent (Ongoing recurring revenue)
1. Lead Generation Begins: Site generates 15-40 qualified leads per month
2. Client Identification: Research local service providers with strong reviews/reputation
3. Free Lead Demonstration: Provide 1 lead completely free as proof of concept
4. Value Proof Phase: Deliver 6-12 additional free leads over 1-2 months
5. Close Sale: Convert to paying client at $800-$1,500/month (average $1,000)
6. Ongoing Service: Continue generating leads, maintain site, provide monthly reports


Revenue per Site: $1,000/month average (month-to-month, recurring)

CUSTOMER ACQUISITION STRATEGY (ZERO CAC)
The Free Lead Method
Step 1: Identify Qualified Providers


* Research local service providers in target market
* Review Google reviews, testimonials, licensing
* Verify service area coverage
* Assess reputation and professionalism
* Create shortlist of 3-5 ideal candidates


Step 2: Initial Outreach


* Contact owner or sales manager directly
* Brief introduction of opportunity
* Offer 1 completely free lead as demonstration
* No strings attached, no commitment required


Step 3: Deliver First Lead


* Provide lead with full contact information
* Include service details and urgency level
* Follow up with both client and end-customer
* Gather feedback and testimonials


Step 4: Extended Value Demonstration


* Continue providing 6-12 additional free leads
* Track conversion rates and job values
* Build relationship and trust
* Demonstrate consistent quality and volume


Step 5: Close Sale


* Present pricing after proving value
* Show ROI calculation from free leads
* Transition to paid monthly service
* Close Rate: 70% (conservative estimate)


Total Cost: Founder's time only (no cash outlay)


Why This Works:


* Removes all risk from client perspective
* Demonstrates value before asking for payment
* Builds trust and relationship
* Proves lead quality and volume
* Client has already experienced ROI before buying
