# OC Wednesday Weekly Accountability Call

**When:** Wednesdays at 8:45am ET
**Duration:** 45 minutes
**WhatsApp:** https://chat.whatsapp.com/C1lY4NfcwhM4OHEhBg8UIi

## What to Prepare Before Each Call

### 1. Gratitude (5 min)
- [ ] Two things I'm grateful for this week

### 2. Wins (8 min)
- [ ] One win from the past week (deal closed, progress on lead, something learned)
- [ ] What momentum am I building on?

### 3. Challenges & Roadblocks (15 min)
- [ ] Biggest challenge I'm facing right now
- [ ] Any stuck deals or leads?
- [ ] Systems/processes/tools I'm struggling with?
- [ ] What support or resources do I need?

### 4. Deal Deep Dive (12 min) — if applicable
- [ ] Deal story (seller motivation, property details)
- [ ] How am I structuring the offer?
- [ ] Exit strategy and risks
- [ ] Need help with underwriting, finding buyer, or raising capital?

### 5. Goals for Next Week (5 min)
- [ ] One specific, measurable, achievable goal
- [ ] How can the group hold me accountable?

---

## Rules to Remember
- Start with gratitude
- No negative talk — reframe challenges as opportunities
- Keep contributions concise
- Confidentiality — what's shared stays in the group
- Support each other — celebrate wins, offer actionable advice
