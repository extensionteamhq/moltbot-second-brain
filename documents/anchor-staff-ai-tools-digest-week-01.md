## üìà Industry Trends to Watch

### 1. AI-Generated Creative Automation (Meta & Google)
**Timeline:** End of 2026  
**Impact:** Advertisers will input product image + budget, and platform AI will:
- Generate creative variations
- Auto-target audiences
- Personalize in real-time (including by geolocation)

**What This Means for Agencies:** Creative strategy becomes more important than execution. Agencies need to shift from "making ads" to "training AI to make ads in the right voice."

### 2. Disappearing Line Between Marketing Automation & CRM
**Trend:** Tools like HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, and Salesforce are merging marketing + sales workflows into unified platforms.  
**Impact:** Agencies need to think holistically about client tech stacks (not just marketing tools).

### 3. Agentic AI for Social Media
**Trend:** Tools moving from "schedule this post" to "here's my brand voice and goals, AI handles posting."  
**Impact:** Social media managers shift from creators to strategists/overseers.

### 4. AI Search Optimization (Beyond Traditional SEO)
**Trend:** With ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google SGE, brands need to optimize for AI-generated answers (not just search results).  
**Tools:** ZipTie.dev, Semrush AI tracking  
**Impact:** New SEO skill required ‚Äî "AI answer optimization."
