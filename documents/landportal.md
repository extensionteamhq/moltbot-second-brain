# Land Portal Research

*Researched: 2026-02-05*

---

## Overview

**Land Portal** (landportal.com) is a real estate data platform built specifically for land investors. Founded by Daniel Apke and Ron Apke (also behind Apke Land), it launched in early 2024 and has quickly become a go-to tool in the land investing space.

**Target Users:** Beginner to experienced land investors, agents, teams, and brokerages specializing in land.

**Availability:** Web app + iOS/Android mobile apps

---

## Core Features

### 1. Land Data & Search
- Proprietary in-house parcel data covering all 50 U.S. states
- Advanced AI-powered filters:
  - Vacant lots detection
  - Wetland coverage
  - Flood-prone areas
  - Landlocked parcels
  - Road frontage information
  - Slope analysis
- Interactive mapping tools with heatmaps showing market activity and demand
- Comprehensive property sidebar with key details and owner info

### 2. Built-In Skip Tracing
- Skip trace by property address
- Provides phone numbers and emails
- DNC (Do Not Contact) scrubbing included
- Bulk skip tracing available on Enterprise plan
- Pricing: $0.05/record on base plans, as low as $0.015/record on Enterprise

### 3. AI-Powered Reports
- **AI Comp Reports:** Estimates current market value by comparing to recent sales
- **Slope Reports:** Assessment of gradient/steepness for development analysis
- **Market Research Reports:** Trends, risks, and investment potential for entire regions

### 4. Nationwide MLS Data
- Access to pending, for sale, and sold land listings
- Details: descriptions, list prices, photos, square footage, days on market, agent info

### 5. Data Export & Storage
- Export/download data and lists
- Data storage for up to 60 days
- Bulk data reports

### 6. Land Portal University (Included)
- Comprehensive land investing course
- Online videos, reading material, downloadable resources
- Live classes and archived webinars
- Instructors are active land investors

## Recommendation

**Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)**

Land Portal looks like a strong upgrade from the current landinsights.co + land.id combo. Key advantages:

1. **All-in-one:** Data, skip tracing, MLS, and education in one platform
2. **AI automation:** Comp reports and slope analysis save hours
3. **Land-specific:** Not retrofitted from generic RE tools

**Suggested Action:**
- Start 7-day free trial on Advanced plan ($99.95/mo)
- Test on a few target counties (AL, GA, TN)
- Compare data quality and skip trace hit rates vs landinsights
- If results are good, could replace both landinsights AND land.id
