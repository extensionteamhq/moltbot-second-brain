# Land Portal (landportal.com) — Deep Dive Research

*Research Date: February 6, 2026*

---

## Overview

Land Portal is a real estate data platform specifically designed for **land investors**. Founded by Daniel and Ron Apke (brothers, seasoned land investors who also run Apke Land). Launched early 2024 and has been growing rapidly in the land investing community.

**Website:** https://landportal.com

---

## Key Features

### 1. Land Data & Lead Generation
- **Nationwide coverage** — All 50 U.S. states, comprehensive parcel data
- **AI-powered vacant land filter** — 99.8% accuracy nationwide
- **Advanced filters:**
  - Landlocked properties
  - Road frontage (min/max)
  - Wetlands coverage
  - FEMA flood zones
  - Slope filters
  - Owner demographics (age, ownership duration)
  - Property type

### 2. Skip Tracing (Built-in)
- Find owner contact info by property address
- Phone numbers and emails
- **Do Not Contact (DNC) scrubbing** included
- Bulk skip tracing available on Enterprise plan

**Skip Tracing Pricing:**
- Starter: $0.05 per record
- Advanced: $0.05 per record  
- Pro: $0.04 per record
- Enterprise: As low as $0.015 per record

### 3. Nationwide MLS Data
- Active, pending, and sold listings
- Property descriptions, list prices, photos
- Days on market, agent info
- Great for running comps

### 4. AI-Powered Reports
- **AI Comp Reports** — Estimates current market value using nearby sales, acreage, location, topography
- **AI Slope Reports** — Assesses gradient/steepness for development potential
- **Market Research Reports** — Trends, risks, investment potential for regions

### 5. Interactive Mapping Tools
- Heatmaps showing market activity and demand
- Visual property exploration
- Overlay data (wetlands, flood, etc.)

### 6. Land Portal University (Included)
- Comprehensive land investing course
- Online videos, reading materials, downloadable resources
- Live classes and recorded webinars
- All instructors are active land investors

### 7. Mobile App
- iOS and Android
- Full functionality matching web app
- Skip trace on the go

## Pros

✅ Purpose-built for land investors (not retrofitted from house flipping tools)  
✅ AI-powered land-specific filters (vacant, landlocked, wetlands, slope)  
✅ Integrated skip tracing with competitive pricing  
✅ Mobile app with full functionality  
✅ Land Portal University included with all plans  
✅ MLS data for running comps  
✅ 30% lifetime recurring affiliate commissions  
✅ Founded by active land investors (credibility)

## Cons

❌ No direct CRM integrations (have to export/import)  
❌ Mapping overlays may have lag or accuracy issues — still need boots-on-ground due diligence  
❌ Pro tier is a big jump ($497/mo) from Advanced ($99.95)  
❌ Data storage limited to 60 days on lower tiers  
❌ Newer platform (launched 2024) — less track record vs. established tools
