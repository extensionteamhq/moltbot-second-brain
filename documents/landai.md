# Land AI Research

*Researched: 2026-02-05*

---

## Overview

**Land AI** (landai.ai) is a fully managed AI cold calling service designed specifically for land investors. Unlike software tools, it's a done-for-you service where their AI ("Maya") handles lead qualification, follow-up, and CRM routing.

**Key Differentiator:** This is NOT software you manage — it's an outsourced acquisitions operation with AI at the core.

---

## How It Works

### The System
1. **You provide:** Lead lists (from your data sources)
2. **They handle:** 
   - Data ingestion & DNC scrubbing
   - Human-dialed calls → warm transfer to AI
   - AI qualification + transcription
   - Human QA review (100% of calls)
   - Comp reports attached
   - CRM routing + nurture follow-up

### Maya (The AI Sales Rep)
- Trained on thousands of land-seller conversations
- Runs professional 5-minute qualification calls
- Extracts: motivation, price expectations, timeline
- Routes leads to:
  - **Hot Lead** → Your CRM with notes, comps, and booking window
  - **Nurture** → Automated follow-up until motivation changes

### Compliance
- Human-dialed first → then AI takes over (TCPA compliant)
- State registrations and bonds filed (TX, UT, etc.)
- Internal DNC registry
- Audit logs and timestamped records
- Works in all 50 states

---

## What You Get

- **~20-30 "Hot" qualified sellers/month** (from 100-200 raw leads)
- **70-80 leads in active nurture** (future pipeline)
- Each hot lead includes:
  - Full conversation transcript
  - AI-verified sentiment analysis
  - Notes on motivation/price/timeline
  - Comp reports
  - Time window for follow-up
- Dedicated Slack workspace with QA lead + account manager

## When Land AI Makes Sense

**Good fit if:**
- You have consistent deal flow and need to scale
- You're spending 20+ hours/week on cold calling
- You value time over cost
- You want to focus on closing, not prospecting
- You have capital from flips to reinvest

**Not ideal if:**
- Just starting out with limited capital
- Doing <10 deals/year
- Want hands-on control of every conversation
- Budget-constrained
