# Land AI (landai.ai) — Deep Dive Research

*Research Date: February 6, 2026*

---

## Overview

Land AI is a **fully managed AI cold calling service** specifically designed for land investors. Unlike software tools you manage yourself, Land AI runs your entire acquisitions front-end — from first contact through qualification — and delivers hot leads directly to your CRM.

**Website:** https://landai.ai

---

## How It Works

### The System (Not Software)

Land AI is positioned as a **managed service**, not a tool you operate. Here's the flow:

1. **You provide leads** — Upload your lead lists (same 100-200 leads you'd buy anywhere)
2. **Human-dialed handoff** — Real humans dial the number first
3. **AI takes over** — Once connected, "Maya" (their AI sales rep) handles the conversation
4. **Qualification happens** — Maya extracts motivation, price expectations, timeline
5. **Human QA review** — Every conversation is reviewed by a human analyst before routing
6. **Leads delivered to CRM** — Hot leads go to your pipeline with full notes, comps, transcripts

### Maya — The AI Sales Rep

- Trained on thousands of land-seller conversations
- Runs professional 5-minute qualification calls
- Extracts motivation, price expectations, timeline
- Routes to you (hot) or nurture (not ready yet)
- Never takes a day off, never forgets details
- Sounds professional, not robotic

## Who Is This For?

**Good fit if:**
- You have capital but limited time
- Hate cold calling or managing VAs
- Want a predictable, scalable acquisitions system
- Already have GoHighLevel or compatible CRM
- Doing 50+ leads/month

**Not a good fit if:**
- Tight budget (likely $500+/mo minimum)
- Want full control over every conversation
- Just starting out with small lead volumes
- Prefer DIY approach

## Recommendation

**Land AI is compelling but likely premium-priced.**

Best approach:
1. **Book a discovery call** to get pricing
2. **Start small** — Try 100-lead plan for one month
3. **Compare ROI** — Track cost per deal vs. current method
4. **Evaluate later** — Once deal flow is proven, decide if it's worth the premium

For Dirt Roamers at current stage, this might be a **Phase 4+ consideration** after:
- GoHighLevel CRM is set up
- Lead generation is dialed in
- You have data on current cost-per-deal to compare
